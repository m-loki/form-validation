ConQuest 2025: CQ University Registration

CQ University offers hands-on training courses tailored for CQ system administrators. These sessions are scheduled for Monday, April 7th, and Tuesday, April 8th, just before the ConQuest User Conference.

  • The cost is $895 per day or $1,600 for both days.
  • Multiple sessions can be selected, but please ensure you do not choose sessions that run concurrently.

Pre-requisites:

  • Familiarity with the basics of Salesforce.
  • A working knowledge of the CQ Product relevant to the training sessions.

Please bring your laptop and power cord. For any questions, email us at conquest@compliancequest.com. We look forward to seeing you there!
